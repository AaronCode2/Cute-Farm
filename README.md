How To Play:
<p>
  <ol>
    <li>Go press Start and if you want to go to the Start Menu press Q.</li>
    <li>Find a place where you would like to grow your farm.</li>
    <li>Once you have found a good place to settle plant your on where the player is by pressing 1 to place Dirt.</li>
    <li>Select a plant to grow by going on the top corner and clicking on a plant icon.</li>
    <li>When standing on that Dirt press 1 to grow the selected plant.</li>
    <li>It will starting growing into stages and soon ripe.</li>
    <li>Once the plant is ripe, harvest it by pressing 2 on the ripe plant it should disappear and you will become Richer!</li>
    <li>Now, press E it will bring a Dealers Menu. This is where you can get stuff for a cost and pay attention to the Scam %.</li>
    <li>Lets say you have 200 in your pockets, use your money to buy the stuff you need and be careful as you will need to use your choices wisely! That's it and then Repeat!</li>
    <li>Most Important: Have Fun</li>
  </ol>
</p>
